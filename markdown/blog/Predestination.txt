Title: Predestination 

Watched *[Predestination][predest]* last night. It was a nifty little time travel puzzle, plus an affecting meditation on the nature of human identity... sort of like a sci-fi version of *Still Alice*. Based on an original story by Robert Heinlein, which I will now have to read.
